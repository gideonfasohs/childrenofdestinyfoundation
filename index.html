About Children of Destiny

Margaret Umburi is the founding member of the Children of Destiny and is a mother figure to all the children. She has previously worked with different orphanages in Tanzania, her enthusiasm and the mutual love between her and the children made the setting up of Children of Destiny possible.
Children of Destiny offers a safe haven for orphaned children and is dedicated to providing education, health care and above all a safe, caring environment where our children can grow up healthy and happy and fulfil each of their destinies.
Children of Destiny is located in the shadow of Kilimanjaro in Moshi, Tanzania. Children of Destiny is currently home to 26, girls and boys. Margaret Mponda is the head of this unusual family and, together with the assistance of care takers and volunteers, provides a safe, stable, family environment within which the children can flourish.
